795. You laid aside Your najesty

You laid aside, Your majesty Gave up everything for me Suffered at the hands of those You had created You took away my guilt and shame When You died and rose again Now today You reign And heaven and earth exalt You I really want to worship You, my Lord You have won my heart and I am Yours Forever and ever, I will love You You are the only one who died for me Gave Your life to set me free So I lift my voice to You in adoration

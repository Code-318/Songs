All I once held dear, built my life upon, all this world reveres and wars to own, all I once thought gain I have counted loss; spent and worthless now, compared to this. Knowing you, Jesus, knowing you; there is no greater thing. You’re my all, you’re the best, you’re my joy, my righteousness and I love you, Lord. 2. Now my heart’s desire is to know you more, to be found in you and known as yours; to possess by faith what I could not earn, all-surpassing gift of righteousness. 3. O to know the power of your risen life and to know you in your sufferings; to become like you in your death, my Lord, so with you to live and never die.

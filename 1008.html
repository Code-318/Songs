1008. The Lord's my shepherd, I'll not want

The Lord's my Shepherd I'll not want He makes me lie in pastures green He leads me by the still still waters His goodness restores my soul And I will trust in You alone And I will trust in You alone For Your endless mercy follows me Your goodness will lead me home He guides my ways in righteousness And He anoints my head with oil And my cup it overflows with joy I feast on His pure delights And though I walk the darkest path I will not fear the evil one For You are with me, and Your rod and staff Are the comfort I need to know

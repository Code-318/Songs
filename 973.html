973. All that I am, I lay before You

All That I Am I Lay Before You; All I Possess, Lord, I Confess Is Nothing Without You. Saviour And King, I Now Enthrone You; Take My Life, My Living Sacrifice To You. Lord, Be The Strength Within My Weakness; Be The Supply In Every Need, That I May Prove Your Promises To Me, Faithful And True In Word And Deed. Into Your Hands I Place The Future; The Past Is Nailed To Calvary, That I May Live In Resurrection Power, No Longer I But Christ In Me.
